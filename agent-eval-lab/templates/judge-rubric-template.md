# Judge Rubric Template

Score from 1 to 5 using observable criteria.

- 5: Fully satisfies the expected behavior with complete evidence and no material issues.
- 4: Mostly satisfies the expected behavior with a minor non-blocking issue.
- 3: Partially satisfies the expected behavior but misses one important requirement.
- 2: Mostly fails the expected behavior and only captures a small part correctly.
- 1: Fails the expected behavior or produces unsupported, unsafe, or unusable output.

Always include evidence and mark judge-sensitive cases for human review.
